project summary
Re-imagining the online shopping experience for Gen-Z:

making shopping social + smarter

As Product Lead at Garde-Robe, I worked directly with the CEO/Founder to imagine the power user — stylish, tech-savvy, and highly influenced by their close social circle.

From here, we designed solutions that help this user find inspiration, organize shopping to make meaningful purchases, ask friends for advice, see what friends are buying, and archive closet pieces & outfits.

A shopping ecosystem.

results summary
95% reduction in sign-up drop-off after onboarding flow redesign
200% increase in engagement after value-prop & feature updates

For Gen-Z, cultural alignment and visual language matter — but so does authenticity.

The goal: create a brand voice for notifications, in-app popups, email, and social — then ensure visuals reinforce that same message.
